Drakantos is a free-to-play MMORPG with a nostalgic pixel art style, combined with exciting fast-paces action combat and more than 20 unique playable heroes.

Explore different gameplay styles! Each hero in Drakantos stands out with their unique mechanics, personality traits, and story roles. Breaking away from traditional MMORPG class systems, our heroes bring a new approach to cooperative gameplay. With each update, we will introduce new heroes, ensuring an ever-evolving landscape of strategies and dynamics for players to explore and dominate.
